Welcome to
Cold Forge

I am Scott Doyle and I am a professional Game Master, I introduce groups to Table Top RPGs such as Dungeons & Dragons or Pathfinder. In these Role Playing Games, players will engage with creative writing and teambuilding excercizes as they create their characters and interact with the world they are placed in.

Prices per session

  • Solo or Duo sessions are $200
  • $300 for four players
  • $500 for groups of five to eight

With Larger groups the complexity increases rapidly, increasing the price.

Why they're so high...

Part of the price is due to the time required to create the world the game is played in. With larger groups it takes even more time as each player adds more stories and connections.
